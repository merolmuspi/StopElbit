Framed as “industrial cooperation” or “security partnerships,” integration of Israeli military technology into European defence infrastructures is getting deeper

18 December 2025 · European–Israeli Arms Trade

Several European NATO countries have ordered hundreds of loitering munitions—often described as suicide drones—through a partnership between the German arms manufacturer Rheinmetall and the Israeli company UVision. Deliveries are scheduled to begin in early 2026.

The systems, marketed under the HERO family of loitering munitions, are produced in Europe by Rheinmetall’s subsidiary RWM Italia, while the weapons technology and munitions are supplied by UVision, an Israeli drone manufacturer.
